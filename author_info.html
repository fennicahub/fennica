3 Author Database

3.1 Description

The author database was created by combining author information from MARC 100 (main authors) and MARC 700 (added authors) fields. Authors were identified primarily through Asteri authority identifiers when available. Records lacking authority identifiers were retained using harmonized author names.

The resulting database represents unique authors appearing in the Fennica dataset and serves as a foundation for author-level analyses and metadata enrichment. Author records were enriched using information obtained from Kanto authority data, including birth and death years, profession, language, country, field of activity, place associations, and related persons. Additional variables include inferred gender, publication activity measures, and the number of Fennica records associated with each author.

Publication activity was calculated from publication years recorded in Fennica and includes the first observed publication year, last observed publication year, mean publication year, and activity span.
